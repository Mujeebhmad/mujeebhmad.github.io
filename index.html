About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Institute of Physics, Polish Academy of Sciences (Warsaw, Poland), specializing in superconducting quantum devices, quantum transport, topological quantum materials, and thermoelectric phenomena.

My research combines advanced thin-film growth, nanofabrication, cryogenic measurements, and quantum device characterization to develop next-generation quantum technologies and energy-efficient electronic systems.

With more than eight years of research experience, I have worked extensively on superconducting Josephson junctions, low-temperature quantum transport, thermoelectric materials, topological semimetals, and nanoscale device physics.
